Subordinate Clause Word Order

Frequent confusion with the 'Verb-Last' rule in German 'weil' and 'dass' constructions.

Improving

Incorrect Form

"...weil ich bin nach Hause gegangen."

Corrected Form

"...weil ich nach Hause gegangen bin."

Rule/Explanation

In subordinate clauses, the conjugated verb must occupy the final position in the sentence. This applies to conjunctions like 'weil', 'dass', 'wenn', and 'ob'.
